About me

I am a Game Developer with a passion in programming and design. Most of the time you can find me behind the computer where I program, design or play games. Other times Im out for a walk, because I love nature and all the inspiration and thoughts I can get during the walk. I don't like when I’m bored, that’s why I’m always creating or crafting someting. I like solving logical problems and trying to find the optimal solutions. I can be easily inspired by interesting and ambitious people.

My main job is programming the games, but sometimes I'm also responsible for art and design.
